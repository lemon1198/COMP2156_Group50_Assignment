Learning about DevOps engineering is increasingly vital in the software development cycle due to its profound impact on the efficiency, reliability, and scalability of software delivery processes. DevOps engineers play a pivotal role in bridging the traditional gap between development and operations teams, fostering collaboration, communication, and automation throughout the software development lifecycle.
Learning DevOps engineering is crucial for software development life cycle (SDLC) as it enhances collaboration. 
DevOps practices streamline development processes, leading to faster delivery, higher quality software, and improved.
