George Brown College is a publicly funded college located inthe heart of  Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It was established in 1967 and named after George Brown, a noted 19th-century journalist, politician, and founder of The Globe newspaper (which later became The Globe and Mail). 
There are many modern facilities offered at the campus like, equipped labs, a modern library and recreational activities for all the students.
George Brown College was founded in 1967 and has since been dedicated to providing quality education.
